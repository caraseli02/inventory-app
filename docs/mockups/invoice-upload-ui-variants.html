Recommended: Progressive Disclosure Pattern

Why it's the best choice for your grocery inventory app:

  1. Tablet-first design - Your app is optimized for tablets, and progressive disclosure works better on smaller screens with clear focus
  2. User profile match - Grocery owners are likely occasional users (not accountants processing 100 invoices/day), so guided workflow prevents errors
  3. Consistency with existing patterns - Matches your current Excel import flow (upload ‚Üí preview ‚Üí confirm), reducing learning curve
  4. Lower cognitive load - Grocery owners are busy running their business; one step at a time reduces overwhelm
  5. Better error prevention - Can't proceed until OCR completes and data is validated, preventing incomplete imports
  6. Industry standard for simplicity - Used by QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks‚Äîapps designed for small business owners, not enterprise accountants

When to consider Single-Page Dashboard: If your user base evolves to process 10+ invoices daily and requests a faster workflow, you can offer it as an "Advanced Mode" toggle.
