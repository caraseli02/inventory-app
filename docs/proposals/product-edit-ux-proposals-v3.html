Why This is Recommended

  • Best for inventory workflows: Markup tier changes are the most frequent edit - having them as quick actions reduces friction significantly.
  • Scalable: Accordion sections can grow with new features without overwhelming users.
  • Tablet-optimized: Works equally well in portrait and landscape without layout changes.
  • Combines benefits: Has the focus of a modal, the context of inline editing, and the organization of tabs.
  • Follows modern patterns: Aligns with Stripe, Linear, and Notion's approach to complex data editing.
